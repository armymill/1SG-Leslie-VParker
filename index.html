1SG Leslie V.Parker- US. Army Special Agent

SG. Leslie V. Parker has recently assumed duties as the First Sergeant staff of the U.S. Army, starting from August 9, 2011. Prior to this, he served as a First sergeant agent of the U.S. Army. Born and raised in FORT WAYNE,IN ,1SG. Leslie is a graduate of the U.S. Military Academy at West Point, New York. He holds a Master of Science in Aerospace Engineering from the Georgia Institute of Technology and was a National Security Fellow at Harvard University Throughout his career, 1SG. Leslie has held various special agent assignments including working with the command chief of staff of the 101st Airborne Division (Air Assault) and as the First Sergeant in Combined Joint Task Force-101 during Operation Enduring Freedom. He became an infantryman and served another yearlong tour in Korea. Leslie served as a squad leader for two years at Fort Hood, Texas, and for two years in Germany. After he graduated school and served over two years as a drill sergeant at Fort Benning, Georgia. He returned to Europe in February 2014 as an operations noncommissioned officer for the 19th Battlefield Coordination Detachment, then served in Germany for two years as a squad leader in the 1st Battalion, 18th Infantry Regiment. Finally, in April 2017, 1SG Leslie vince served as a platoon sergeant in the 3rd Brigade, 3rd Infantry Division, at Fort Benning, Georgia.
